Shyam M Saladi about me...

I am a biophysicist and engineer excited about using computation to solve large-scale problems in the life sciences.

Currently, I am a NSF graduate research fellow at Caltech. Bil Clemons and I are developing computer-driven methods to streamline the experimental process of biochemistry research. We aim to increase efficiency at the lab-bench by helping scientists design more tailored experiments.

At Illinois, I was fortunate to work with Claudio Grosman and Eric Jakobsson on the physiology and evolution of ion channels while obtaining my Bachelor degrees in Electrical Engineering and Molecular and Cellular Biology.

In my spare time, you might find me acculmating stamps in my passport, exploring the natural landscapes of California, or pulling apart the nearest electronic item.
